    \noindent $\because 9876543\pmod5 = 3$

    by Modular Arithmetic Properties, we can have:
    \begin{align*}
        & 9876543^{12}\pmod 5 \\
        & = 3^{12}\pmod 5\\ 
        & = 81^3\pmod 5 \\
        & = (81\mod5)^3 \\
        & = 1
    \begin{align*}
